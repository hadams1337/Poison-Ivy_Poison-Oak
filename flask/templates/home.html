What do Poison Ivy and Poison Oak look like?

The purpose of this app is to make YOU familiar with what poison ivy and poison oak look like.

1) Put in your region and the season and I'll show you what to look for!

2) Take a pic of a suspicious plant using your new knowledge

3) Head over to the Upload page and upload your picture, I'll tell you whether the plant is poison ivy, poison oak or benign!
